ShyamPracticePaper is a dedicated platform designed to empower students preparing for competitive exams such as GATE, JEE, NEET, and state-level entrance tests. We offer a mix of free and paid resources including real-time mock tests, downloadable notes, video tutorials, and topic-wise practice questions to help learners prepare smartly and efficiently.

Our platform offers expert-designed content tailored for each exam. You’ll benefit from:
  • Real-time mock test environments to simulate actual exams
  • Topic-wise, subject-wise, and year-wise practice sets
  • Interactive learning materials crafted by educators
  • Both free and affordable paid premium content

You can access multiple courses and mock test series simultaneously. Our flexible system lets you combine study materials across different subjects and exams — whether you're targeting a specific subject like Physics or brushing up on full-syllabus tests.

Just click the “Sign Up” button at the top-right of the page. You can register using your email address or sign in with your Google account. Once logged in, you can browse through:
  • Free resources: practice sets, mock exams, and revision materials
  • Premium resources: full courses, previous year paper sets, topic-wise quizzes
  • Mock test shop: pick mock tests by exam type, topic, year, or difficulty
